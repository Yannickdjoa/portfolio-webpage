About me

DeFi expert // Web3 developer Founder, Investor & Advisor // sharing my finance experience with those building Web3

I am a Europe based Web3 & Blockchain developer, founder, investor, and advisor. An happy full-time father of 4 kids.

I started my journey in web development in 2021, coming from finance field. For over 13+ years as a finance manager, I have led teams to establish finance process and ensure a smooth business development in a wide variety of global industries.

I am currently contributing to building an inclusive decentralised finance in the world as the Founder of Goos Finance, launching the first "decentralised Dapp connecting Defi with cash". The project allows people around the world to earn money by processing financial operations such as given or picking money from digital asset’s trader/holders.

As an advisor to, and investor in, Blockchain and Web3 companies, I help companies grow by aligning their product, marketing, and technology roadmaps with business objectives and consumer demand to develop go-to-market strategies and to create seamless, engaging experiences for users who might not yet be fully comfortable with Web3 and blockchain technology.

Let's chat! I have nothing to sell. If you are interested in or building Web3 / Blockchain / Crypto I'd love to connect!
